3. Data Collection on This Website

Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use a cookie consent management tool (Cookiebot) that gives you the option to decide on the use of cookies when you first visit.

Necessary cookies: These are set without your consent as they are required for the operation of the website (e.g., storing your cookie settings).

Statistics cookies: These are only set with your explicit consent and serve to analyze website usage (Google Analytics).

Cookie Consent with Cookiebot

Our website uses Cookiebot's cookie consent technology to obtain your consent for storing certain cookies and to document this in a data protection-compliant manner. The provider is Cybot A/S, Havnegade 39, 1058 Copenhagen, Denmark.

When you visit our website, a Cookiebot cookie is stored in your browser, documenting the consents you have given or the revocation of those consents. This data is not passed on to the website operator.

Processing is based on Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. c GDPR. The website operator is legally obliged to be able to demonstrate user consent.

Server Log Files

The website provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files that your browser automatically transmits. These are:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL
  • Host name of the accessing computer
  • Time of the server request
  • IP address

This data is not merged with other data sources. Collection is based on Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

4. Analytics Tools

Google Analytics 4

This website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies that enable analysis of your use of the website.

Google Analytics is only activated if you have consented to the "Statistics" category in the cookie banner. Without your consent, no analytics cookies are set and no data is transmitted to Google.

We use the IP anonymization feature. This means your IP address is truncated by Google within member states of the European Union. Only in exceptional cases is the full IP address transmitted to a Google server in the USA and truncated there.

Usage is based on your consent pursuant to Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. a GDPR. You can revoke your consent at any time by changing your cookie settings via the cookie banner.
